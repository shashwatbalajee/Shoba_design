42 stories in 42 months! What an achievement by the contracts team!
❮ ❯

"We are delighted to share that the Contracts Team recently handed over the Azim Premji University (APU) Hostel and Dining Blocks. With the commitment of timely delivery and world-class quality, SOBHA has bolstered the trust of its patron and delivered the project in 42 months. About APU Located on the outskirts of Sarjapur-Attibele Road, APU is spread across over 80 acres out of which over 36 acres is being developed by SOBHA. The area includes two clusters. Cluster-1 comprises five academic blocks with seven levels each, a welcome centre with two floors, a knowledge centre with seven floors, two conference rooms, and a 17-storey executive hostel and kitchen block. Cluster-2 comprises the 42-storied hostel, including the cafeteria and kitchen. This learning and living facility has been built with cutting-edge technology and is also environmentally friendly."

SOBHA's CSR arm hosts 28th edition of dowry-free wedding

"In its journey towards making meaningful impact on the lives of the underprivileged, SOBHA's Corporate Social Responsibility (CS) arm - Sri Kurumba Educational and Charitable Trust - recently hosted the 28th edition of the dowry-free social wedding at Palakkad, Kerala on June 17th, 2023 The Trust organizes such social weddings twice a year. As part of the first lot of weddings in 2023, 17 couples tied the knot at the Sri Kurumba Kalyana Mandapam in Moolamkode. To mark the matrimonial ceremony, Mrs. Sobha Menon gifted each couple 4.5 gold sovereign jewellery, along with basic resources such as clothes and utensils to enable the couples to embark on a new journey. To ensure a healthy and sustained marriage, like always, the Trust provided pre- marriage counselling to all couples and their parents. In addition, regular post marriage follow-ups and assistance will also be provided to the couples as required. The function was attended by distinguished guests, including Ms. R. Bindu, Minister for Higher Education and Social Justice; Mr. K. Krishnan Kutty, Minister for Electricity; Mr. K.E. Ismail (Former Minister for Revenue, Kerala Government); Mr. V.C. Kabeer (Former Minister for Health, Kerala Government); and Mr. K.D. Prasenan (MLA, Alathur Legislative Assembly). It also included a feast for 1000+ people. To date, the Trust has organized 684 dowry-free weddings."

Who we are

World Architecture Day | Celebrating our superb architects!

Today we specially acknowledge the contribution of our colleagues from Architecture, who have helped create some marvelous designs and structures, and established new international benchmarks. Thank you for your effort and passion in delivering excellence and happiness to our customers. The life of an architect is challenging, as changing customer needs, working with budgets, constraints on space, local byelaws, and more importantly, innovating new concepts that can be executed is always demanding. In our journey of 27 years of excellence, we have set new benchmarks in international quality and experience. The design studio has delivered 56 mn. sft. built-up area (BUA) across India in collaboration with an incredible talent pool of MEP and structural engineers, 3D vizualizers and miniature model makers. Another 41 mn. sft. of BUA are in various stages of implementation.

Know Your SOBHA - The Environmental Engineering Department

"SOBHA is one of the few companies to invest deeply in the compliance, design, and execution of environmental services for all its projects. We also have a dedicated physicochemical and microbiological laboratory to test water and wastewater at our project sites. We take pride in being an environmentally meticulous company. We conduct feasibility studies before finalising a project to ensure sustainable development, a healthy environment for residents, and work towards being compliant with environmental regulations."
